About:

Freeflight - a 3D flight simulator for JAVA (j2me) compatible mobile phones. It will give you a chance to fly with four different aircrafts over many landscapes. The 3d graphics are taking alot of the phone resources, thats why you need some powerfull Symbian phone to have it running smoothly....

Free Flight game features 3D:

Various scenery

Various models of planes

Full 3D graphics

Different physics of planes
